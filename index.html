About Hon. Newtoh Kajunior

Hon. Newtoh Kajunior is a dedicated and compassionate leader whose journey is defined by a deep commitment to serving his community. As a practicing nurse, he has witnessed firsthand the healthcare challenges faced by many families in Akachiu,Ward. This experience has fueled his passion to improve living standards and promote well-being for all.

Beyond his medical career, Newtoh has emerged as a prominent community mobilizer and politician. He believes in a collaborative approach to development, where every voice is heard and every person is valued. His vision extends beyond politics; he aims to build a future where young people have opportunities to thrive and where the elderly are cared for with dignity.

He is a strong advocate for **education, healthcare, and youth empowerment**, believing these pillars are essential for a prosperous society. His leadership is guided by principles of integrity, transparency, and a relentless drive to make a positive impact.

My Vision & Core Values

My vision is to create a vibrant and prosperous community in Akachiu,Ward, where every individual has the opportunity to achieve their full potential. This vision is built on a foundation of core values that guide every action and decision I make.

"The future belongs to those who believe in the beauty of their dreams. Together, we can build a stronger, healthier, and more empowered community."

Integrity

Upholding honesty and moral principles in all dealings, ensuring trust and accountability with the people.

Unity

Bringing people together to work towards a common goal, fostering a sense of belonging and collective strength.

Compassion

Demonstrating empathy and care for the vulnerable, and advocating for the well-being of every community member.

My Work and Campaigns

Community Outreach & Development

My work is driven by a commitment to grassroots development. I have initiated numerous projects focusing on youth empowerment, improving local infrastructure, and providing essential services to the most vulnerable members of our community. I believe in a bottom-up approach to development, where the needs of the people are at the forefront of every decision.

Healthcare & Wellness

As a nurse, I understand the importance of accessible healthcare. I have organized medical camps, health awareness seminars, and partnered with local health facilities to ensure our community receives quality medical attention. My goal is to make healthcare a right, not a privilege.

Youth & Talent Empowerment

I am passionate about nurturing the next generation. My campaigns have focused on providing mentorship, facilitating access to vocational training, and creating platforms for young people to showcase their talents and skills, ensuring they have a promising future.
