About Me

Newtoh Kajunior (Kiriinya) is a dedicated healthcare professional and passionate public servant from Maua, Kenya. With a deep-rooted commitment to improving his community, he combines his medical expertise with political leadership to create meaningful change.

Born and raised in the Meru region, Newtoh understands the challenges faced by his people firsthand. His journey from humble beginnings to professional success serves as an inspiration to many young Kenyans. He believes in empowering the youth, ensuring access to quality healthcare, and fostering sustainable local development.

Vision & Mission

My vision is to create a community where every individual has the opportunity to thrive, with access to quality healthcare, education, and economic opportunities. My mission is to champion policies and initiatives that foster sustainable growth, social equity, and community well-being in Maua and beyond.

"Faith in God, hope for the future, and unwavering service to the community - these are the pillars that guide my leadership and commitment to a brighter tomorrow for all."
